Abstract

This webpage provides supplemental comparison videos for our work on Gaussian Splatting. Our method demonstrates high-fidelity 3D reconstruction and rendering from sparse multi-view images. Below, you can find flythroughs and motion comparisons against state-of-the-art techniques.

Method Overview

Gaussian Splatting models scenes as collections of 3D Gaussians that are rendered using a real-time rasterization pipeline. Our approach enables fast training and high-quality rendering. Please refer to our paper and GitHub repository for more details.
